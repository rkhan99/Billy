About Us


Billy’s was incepted in the latter part of 2019 when two long-time friends, Behram and Husbaan, came up with the idea to use top quality peanuts from Parachinar, Pakistan, to manufacture peanut butter and introduce it in the local market. Combining the professional expertise of Behram and the experience of Husbaan, they formed Billy’s with the aim of making it one of the biggest nut butter manufacturing brands in the world.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide top standard nut butter to our customers that is made using highest quality nuts, under optimum hygiene standards and made with lots of love


Vision

Our vision is to become the best nut butter manufacturing company in Pakistan and one of the leading food manufacturing company in the world.


Behram Khan

An ex-professional cricketer representing Pakistan Under-19s and then going on to play 10 years of First-Class cricket, holds a degree of Bachelors in Commerce and is a double masters, having done a Masters in Economics and recently completed his Masters in Business Administration (MBA) from Cardiff University (UK) acquiring a distinction. He also has professional work experience working in financial institutions, real estate and for K-Electric, a large-scale power generation, transmission and distribution company.

Husbaan Javed

An ex-professional cricketer having represented Karachi in First class cricket, holds a Bachelors degree in Commerce. He joined his 50-year-old family business of food manufacturing. Initially worked under the guidance of his father, Husbaan holds ample experience of manufacturing and marketing after being in the field for about 5 years.
